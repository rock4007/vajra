# Privacy Policy - Vajra Kavach Emergency Response System

**Effective Date**: January 29, 2026  
**Last Updated**: January 29, 2026

## 1. Introduction

Vajra Kavach ("we", "our", "us") operates an emergency response system designed to protect users during critical situations. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your personal information.

**Your privacy is critical to us.** We only collect the minimum data necessary to provide emergency services and ensure your safety.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Personal Information
- **Identity Data**: Name, email address, phone number
- **Location Data**: GPS coordinates (only during emergencies)
- **Biometric Data**: Fingerprint hash (cryptographic representation only - never raw biometric data)
- **Device Data**: Device type, OS version, app version
- **Emergency Data**: Audio recordings, video recordings (during emergency activation)

### 2.2 Automatically Collected Information
- IP address
- Login timestamps
- Usage patterns
- Error logs
- Network information

### 2.3 Information We DO NOT Collect
- Raw biometric data (fingerprints stored as one-way cryptographic hashes)
- Credit card numbers (if applicable, tokenized only)
- Social security numbers
- Medical records (unless voluntarily provided for emergency services)
- Browsing history outside our application

## 3. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

We process your data based on:

### 3.1 Consent
You explicitly consent to data processing for emergency services when you create an account.

### 3.2 Legitimate Interest
Emergency response and user safety constitute legitimate interests.

### 3.3 Legal Obligation
Compliance with law enforcement requests and regulatory requirements.

### 3.4 Vital Interest
Processing necessary to protect your life or physical safety during emergencies.

## 4. How We Use Your Information

### 4.1 Primary Uses
- **Emergency Response**: Dispatch help to your location
- **Identity Verification**: Confirm your identity during emergencies
- **Service Improvement**: Enhance emergency response times
- **Security**: Detect and prevent fraud, abuse, security incidents

### 4.2 We DO NOT
- Sell your personal data to third parties
- Use your data for marketing without explicit consent
- Share data with advertisers
- Track you for non-emergency purposes

## 5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

### 5.1 Emergency Services
During an active emergency, we share:
- Your name
- Location (GPS coordinates)
- Emergency type
- Contact information

**Recipients**: Police, ambulance, fire services, designated emergency contacts

### 5.2 Law Enforcement
We may disclose information when:
- Required by valid legal warrant or subpoena
- Necessary to prevent imminent harm
- Investigating potential crimes
- Complying with court orders

### 5.3 Service Providers
Limited data shared with:
- Cloud hosting providers (AWS/GCP/Azure)
- SMS/notification services
- Payment processors (if applicable)

**All providers are contractually bound to protect your data.**

### 5.4 We DO NOT Share With
- Marketing companies
- Data brokers
- Social media platforms
- Advertisers

## 6. Data Retention

### 6.1 Active Users
- Personal data retained while account is active
- Location data retained for 90 days
- Emergency recordings retained for 1 year (legal requirement)

### 6.2 Account Deletion
Upon account deletion:
- Personal data deleted within 30 days
- Backups purged within 90 days
- Legal records retained as required by law (typically 1-7 years)

### 6.3 Audit Logs
- Security logs retained for 1 year
- Access logs retained for 180 days (CERT-In requirement)

## 7. Your Privacy Rights

### 7.1 Right to Access (GDPR Article 15)
Request a copy of all personal data we hold about you.

**How to exercise**: Email privacy@vajrakavach.com with subject "Data Access Request"

### 7.2 Right to Rectification (GDPR Article 16)
Correct inaccurate or incomplete data.

**How to exercise**: Update via app settings or email privacy@vajrakavach.com

### 7.3 Right to Erasure / Right to be Forgotten (GDPR Article 17)
Request deletion of your personal data.

**How to exercise**: Email privacy@vajrakavach.com with subject "Data Deletion Request"

**Exceptions**: Legal obligations, ongoing investigations, vital safety interests

### 7.4 Right to Restrict Processing (GDPR Article 18)
Limit how we use your data.

### 7.5 Right to Data Portability (GDPR Article 20)
Export your data in JSON format for transfer to another service.

### 7.6 Right to Object (GDPR Article 21)
Object to processing based on legitimate interests.

### 7.7 Rights Under CCPA (California Residents)
- Right to know what personal information is collected
- Right to delete personal information
- Right to opt-out of sale (we don't sell data)
- Right to non-discrimination

### 7.8 Rights Under Indian IT Act
- Right to correction
- Right to information disclosure
- Right to compensation for data breaches

## 8. Data Security Measures

### 8.1 Encryption
- **In Transit**: TLS 1.3 (HTTPS)
- **At Rest**: AES-256-GCM encryption
- **Biometrics**: SHA-256 one-way hashing (irreversible)
- **Passwords**: Argon2id hashing

### 8.2 Access Controls
- Multi-factor authentication (MFA) required
- Role-based access control (RBAC)
- Principle of least privilege
- Session timeout after 30 minutes inactivity

### 8.3 Monitoring
- 24/7 security monitoring
- Intrusion detection systems
- Real-time threat analysis
- Automated breach detection

### 8.4 Compliance Certifications
- ISO/IEC 27001:2022 (Information Security)
- SOC 2 Type II compliant
- GDPR compliant
- PCI DSS compliant (if processing payments)

## 9. International Data Transfers

### 9.1 Data Localization
Sensitive data for Indian users stored within India (compliance with data localization requirements).

### 9.2 Cross-Border Transfers
For EU users, data transfers use:
- Standard Contractual Clauses (SCC)
- Adequacy decisions recognized by EU Commission

### 9.3 Data Residency
You can request data to be stored in specific regions (subject to availability).

## 10. Children's Privacy

**Age Restriction**: 13 years minimum (16 in EU)

We do not knowingly collect data from children under the minimum age. If we discover such data, it will be deleted immediately.

**Parental Consent**: Required for users under 18 (varies by jurisdiction).

## 11. Data Breach Notification

### 11.1 Our Commitment
If a data breach occurs, we will:
1. Contain the breach immediately
2. Investigate within 48 hours
3. Notify supervisory authorities within 72 hours (GDPR requirement)
4. Notify affected users without undue delay
5. Provide breach details, consequences, and remediation steps

### 11.2 User Notification
You will be notified via:
- Email (to registered address)
- In-app notification
- SMS (for critical breaches)
